Welcome to AthleticaX

AthleticaX automates the entire sports lifecycle—from eligibility rules to result publishing—securely and at scale. Here is how our three-tier, role-based platform empowers athletes, coaches, and administrators:

Automated Eligibility

Say goodbye to manual checks. Our decoupled rules engine automatically evaluates age, gender, weight, and height to classify registrations as auto-qualified, review-needed, or ineligible.

Coach–Athlete Workflow

Athletes can directly request coaching assistance through their portal. Coaches can manage their profiles, accept or reject requests, and mentor their assigned athletes effectively.

Unified Event Lifecycle

Administrators govern a streamlined lifecycle. Create meets, define custom eligibility criteria per event, and seamlessly transition events from Upcoming to Ongoing to Completed.

Bulk Short-Listing

Admins can efficiently process bulk short-listing of qualified candidates and instantly publish transparent, online results accessible to both athletes and coaches.

Defense-in-Depth Security

Your data is protected by a robust security baseline featuring stateless JWT sessions, OTP-verified e-mail sign-ups, BCrypt password hashing, and strict role-based access.

Role-Specific Portals

Experience an intuitive, responsive interface built with HTML5, Bootstrap 5.3, and JS. Each role (Admin, Athlete, Coach) gets a dedicated portal showing only the data they need.
