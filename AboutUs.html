About AthleticaX

Welcome to AthleticaX! We recognized a significant bottleneck in our college's sports management: event creation, athlete registration, shortlisting, and result publication rely heavily on slow, error-prone manual paperwork and scattered spreadsheets. To solve this, final-year Computer Science and Engineering students at St. Thomas' College of Engineering and Technology, under the expert guidance of Prof. Suparna Dutta, developed AthleticaX. Our secure, role-based platform digitizes the entire sports lifecycle—from preventing duplicate entries to automating eligibility checks—bringing efficiency, transparency, and a centralized source of truth to sports event management.
